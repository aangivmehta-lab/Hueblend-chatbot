Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Hueblend, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect when you use the Hueblend Brand Diagnostic tool, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. What we collect

When you use the Brand Diagnostic, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address (only if you choose to submit the lead form)
  • Your brand type and the problem category identified in your session
  • A transcript of your conversation with the AI advisor
  • The date and time of your session
  • Whether you clicked the "Book a strategy call" CTA

We also store a local memory in your browser (using localStorage) so that returning users get a personalised experience. This data never leaves your device unless you submit the lead form.

2. How we use your data

  • To provide you with your diagnosis and follow-up recommendations
  • To improve the quality of the Hueblend Advisor over time
  • To follow up with you about Hueblend services (only if you submit your email)
  • To understand common brand challenges across our user base

3. What we never do

  • We never sell your data to third parties
  • We never share your personal information with advertisers
  • We never use your data to train AI models without your explicit consent
  • We never send spam — only relevant follow-ups if you opted in

4. Data storage

If you submit the lead form, your data is stored in a secured Google Sheet accessible only to the Hueblend team. We use Google's infrastructure, which complies with industry-standard security practices.

Your browser memory (localStorage) is stored entirely on your own device and is never transmitted to our servers unless you submit the form.

5. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Ask us to delete your data at any time
  • Opt out of follow-up communications
  • Clear your local browser memory by clearing your browser's localStorage

6. AI disclaimer

The Hueblend Advisor is an AI-powered tool designed to provide general marketing insights and direction. It is not a substitute for professional marketing, legal, or financial advice. Hueblend is not liable for business decisions made based on the AI's output.

7. Cookies

This tool does not use tracking cookies. We use localStorage (a browser-based storage mechanism) solely to remember returning users and improve their experience.

Questions about your privacy? Reach us at hello@hueblend.com — we respond within 48 hours.
